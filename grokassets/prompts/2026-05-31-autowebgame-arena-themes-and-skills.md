# Prompt — AutoWebGame Additional Arena Themes + Skill Visualizations

**Project**: AutoWebGame
**Asset Types**: Arena environment illustrations, skill/effect visuals
**Priority**: High

## Visual Bible Reference
Themed arenas: arcane (purples/magentas), verdant (greens), skyfoundry (blues/oranges). Clean tournament readability. Beautiful skill effects that are not noisy. Four distinct characters.

## Exact Prompts (generate 2-3 arena + 2-3 skill)

### Arcane Arena Theme
Beautifully detailed arcane-themed Bomberman arena: glowing purple/magenta runes on the grid, mystical bomb placements, high readability, clean lines. Tournament feel with arcane flair. Excellent for marketing and in-game reference.

### Verdant / Skyfoundry Arena
Lush green verdant arena or industrial skyfoundry (blues/oranges) with strong silhouette readability for characters and bombs. Procedural but hand-crafted beauty.

### Signature Skill Visualization
One powerful, beautiful skill effect for each of the four characters (e.g., Ranni's elegant ability, Killer Bee's aggressive pattern, Nico's crocodilian move, Crocodilo Arcano's arcane bomb). Clean, readable, game-y but artistic.
