# Visual Bible — AutoWebGame (BOMBA)

**Core Metaphor**: Fast, skill-based Bomberman arena with personality. Four signature characters (Ranni, Killer Bee, Nico, Crocodilo Arcano) with distinct visual and mechanical identities. Clean tournament arena that can transform into arcane, verdant, skyfoundry themes. Browser-first competitive local + online play.

**Emotional Promise**: Pure, joyful, skill-expressive arena combat with memorable characters and beautiful, readable arenas.

**Visual DNA Keywords**: Classic Bomberman DNA evolved, distinct character silhouettes, procedural clean arenas with strong readability, skill effects that are beautiful not noisy, four signature heroes with strong personality, "BOMBA" as explosive but controlled energy.

**Color System**:
- Tournament clean default: crisp, high contrast, low visual noise
- Themed arenas: arcane (purples/magentas), verdant (greens), skyfoundry (blues/oranges)
- Character-specific accent colors

**Typography Direction**: Bold, game-y but readable. "BOMBA" in explosive but elegant display type.

**Audience & Cultural Notes**: Fans of classic arena games who want modern browser + online + character skills. Quick matches, personality, readability.

**Forbidden Styles**:
- Overly realistic 3D (this is 2D arena with soul)
- Generic mobile game candy crush colors

## Reference Assets

Logo: "BOMBA" with one of the signature characters or a perfectly composed bomb + arena grid that feels iconic.

Character illustrations and arena heroes for marketing.
